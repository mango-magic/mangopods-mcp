## Scoring Rubric

Score each application out of 100.

| Category | Points | What to look for |
| --- | ---: | --- |
| Reach | 25 | Audience, customers, clients, community, distribution, or internal user base |
| Use-case quality | 25 | Clear workflow with obvious MangoPods fit and strong outcome |
| Ability to ship | 20 | Can test in July, has technical owner, has source assets |
| Revenue / credit potential | 15 | Likely recurring credits, resale, bundled workflows, or agency margin |
| Proof value | 10 | Would make a strong public story, demo, or launch example |
| Feedback quality | 5 | Thoughtful applicant likely to help shape the product |

## Access Tiers

- Tier 1 - Founder access: 80-100 points. High reach, strong workflow, July testing capacity, clear proof value.
- Tier 2 - Builder access: 60-79 points. Strong technical or product use case, but smaller reach or less immediate distribution.
- Tier 3 - Partner watchlist: 40-59 points. Interesting agency, studio, or partner model that needs more qualification.
- Tier 4 - Later waitlist: under 40 points. Curious but not yet ready to test or distribute.

## Follow-Up Routing

- Vibe coders: ask what agent/app they want to connect first and what source asset they will test.
- Agencies: ask what repeatable client workflow they want to sell and how they price it today.
- Product teams: ask where MangoPods would sit in the product journey and what action users would click.
- Creators and studios: ask what back catalogue, podcast, webinar, or show workflow they can test in July.
- Partner apps: ask whether they want BYO credits, bundled credits, resale, or white-label creative jobs.
